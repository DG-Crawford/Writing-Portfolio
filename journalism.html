Journalism/feature stories

Context

Wrote feature stories, interviewed public officials and figures involved in major events, and proofread other writers' work.

Problem

The Paisano, an independent newspaper that serves the University of Texas at San Antonio's community, needed writing and editing services. It was a small team, so the newspaper often needed help. Many stories contained typos and inconsistencies before I reached out to assist them.

Contribution

I worked on every newspaper published, sometimes until midnight, to meticulously proof every word in every sentence. I wrote feature stories that others may not have wanted to work on, so I filled a multiple-hat role. Still, I often proofread stories and helped other editors adhere to the AP Style.

Impact

  • Managed a group of 10-15 writers and editors who reduced publication errors.
  • Established a cleawr voice for the publication by developing a style guide.
  • Contibuted to a 50% growth in social media engagement through published stories.
